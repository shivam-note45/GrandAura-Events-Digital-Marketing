# Digital Marketing Project – GrandAura Events

This project presents a complete digital marketing strategy for **GrandAura Events**, a premium event management company operating in metro cities.

## Objectives
- Generate high-quality wedding & corporate event leads
- Build a premium luxury brand image
- Reduce dependency on referrals
- Achieve ROI within 3 months

## Brand Overview
- Brand Name: GrandAura Events
- Industry: Event Management
- Experience: 4 Years
- Services: Weddings, Corporate Events, Private Parties, Decor, Vendor Coordination
- Target Market: Metro cities & nearby areas

## Platforms Used
- Instagram & Facebook
- Google Search Ads
- Website Landing Pages
- WhatsApp Automation

## Time Frame
90 Days (3 Months)

## Outcome
This project demonstrates practical application of digital marketing concepts such as funnel building, paid advertising, content strategy, and lead qualification.
## Brand Brief – GrandAura Events

GrandAura Events is a premium event management company specializing in weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations.

### Services Offered
- Wedding planning & execution
- Corporate events
- Birthday & private parties
- Decor & theme setup
- Vendor coordination

### Price Range
- Small events: ₹1,50,000 – ₹3,00,000
- Large events & weddings: ₹8,00,000+

### Brand Personality
Luxury, Reliable, Elegant, Stress-free, Professional
## Target Audience

### Wedding Clients
- Age: 25–40
- Profile: Couples & families
- Income: Upper-middle to high income
- Needs: Luxury decor, stress-free execution, social status

### Corporate Clients
- Age: 30–55
- Profile: HR Heads, Admin Managers, Founders
- Needs: Professional execution, timely delivery, brand image
## Competitor Analysis

### Competitor Types
- Local wedding planners
- Luxury boutique event companies
- Freelance decorators

### Weaknesses of Competitors
- Poor digital presence
- No clear pricing
- Weak lead follow-up

### Opportunity for GrandAura
- Premium positioning
- Strong Instagram presence
- Professional funnel & automation
## Brand Positioning

GrandAura Events is positioned as a **premium, luxury-focused event management brand** that delivers seamless and stress-free experiences for high-end clients.
## Marketing Objectives

- Generate 25–40 qualified leads per month
- Increase brand visibility among premium audiences
- Convert at least 2–4 clients per month
## Funnel Strategy

Awareness → Trust → Lead Capture → Qualification → Conversion

- Awareness: Instagram Reels & Ads
- Trust: Testimonials & past work
- Lead Capture: Forms & WhatsApp
- Qualification: Budget & date filters
## Platform Strategy

- Instagram: Brand showcase & engagement
- Facebook Ads: Lead generation
- Google Ads: High-intent searches
- Website: Conversion-focused landing pages
